About page

This site is themed after the pokemon world. Pokémon(an abbreviation for Pocket Monsters in Japan) is a Japanese media franchise managed by The Pokémon Company, a company founded by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures. In Pokémon, humans, known as Pokémon Trainers, catch and train Pokémon to battle other Pokémon for sport. All media works within the franchise are set in the Pokémon universe. There are currently 913 Pokémon species.

The site's intention is to grant people all over the world the access to post their own fan arts. They can search fan arts, look at a specific type of fan art and much more.
